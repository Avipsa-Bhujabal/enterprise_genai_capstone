Company: NexaTech Solutions

Remote Work Policy:
Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 3 days per week. Full-time remote work requires manager approval.

Leave Policy:
Employees receive 20 paid leave days annually. Sick leave is capped at 10 days.

Security Policy:
All employees must use VPN when accessing company systems remotely. Sharing credentials is strictly prohibited.

Expense Policy:
Employees can claim up to $1000 annually for work-related equipment.
